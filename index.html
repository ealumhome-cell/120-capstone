Founder & CEO, The OCD Guys | Premium Aircraft Detailing

A native of Houston, Texas, Ashton L. Ealum embodies the intersection of military precision and technical excellence. As the founder and visionary behind "The OCD Guy," Houston’s premier aircraft detailing service, Ashton has redefined the standards of aviation maintenance through a philosophy of uncompromising exactness.

Ashton’s professional foundation was built over 23 years of distinguished service in the United States Navy. This extensive military tenure instilled in him a "mission-first" discipline and an innate respect for high-value assets. Parallel to his service, he excelled as a Data Systems Engineer, a role that honed his analytical mindset and mastered the art of complex problem-solving—traits that now serve as the backbone of his detailing operations.

"The OCD Guys" is the culmination of Ashton’s lifelong commitment to detail and his deep roots in the Houston community. By merging his engineering background with elite-tier preservation techniques, he provides aircraft owners with more than just a cleaning service; he delivers a technical restoration of aesthetic and structural integrity.

Today, Ashton remains dedicated to elevating the aviation industry in his hometown, ensuring that every aircraft under his care reflects the same honor, courage, and commitment he practiced throughout his military and engineering career.
