Aviation Detailing Packages

Bronze Package

$1,500.00 USD

A basic deep cleaning for involves a systematic "top-to-bottom" approach, starting with securing critical sensors like pitot tubes and static ports before a low-pressure exterior wash using pH-neutral aviation soap. The belly and landing gear require specialized degreasers to remove hydraulic fluid and carbon buildup, while windows are hand-cleaned with ammonia-free solutions to prevent crazing. Inside, the process centers on vacuuming high-traffic areas and wiping down wood veneers and leather with soft cotton cloths and mild, approved cleaners. Avionics screens receive a delicate touch with dedicated anti-reflective wipes, and the galley and lavatory are sanitized with non-corrosive disinfectants to protect fine metal plating.

Silver Package

$3,000.00 USD

An advanced aviation detail bridges the gap between a basic wash and a full restoration by focusing on surface decontamination and protection. While a basic cleaning covers a "wet wash" or "dry wash" of the airframe and windows, an advanced service includes mechanical clay bar treatment to remove embedded contaminants, followed by a machine polish or high-grade sealant application to restore paint depth.

Platinum Package

$5,000.00 USD

A premium aviation detailing service for small craft represents the highest tier of aircraft preservation, transforming the plane into a showroom-quality asset while providing maximum protection. Building on advanced decontamination, a premium package prioritizes restoration and long-term shielding, often featuring a multi-stage machine paint correction to eliminate oxidation and swirl marks, followed by the application of a high-end ceramic coating. The exterior work includes high-level brightwork polishing to restore leading edges, engine inlets, and spinners to a mirror-like finish. Internally, the service goes beyond deep cleaning to include specialized leather restoration or re-dyeing, wood veneer polishing, and electrostatic disinfection to ensure a surgically sterile and aesthetically flawless cabin.
